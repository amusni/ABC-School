Q: How do we apply?

A: Prepare the documentary requirements, fill out the online application form, wait for the email from our team for further instructions.

Q: Do we go to the campus to submit the requirements?

A: No need. Just upload the documents (.jpeg/pdf) on the online application form. all further instructions will be given through email. Furthermore, the campus does not let parents and guest inside as of March 2022.

Q: What if I have lacking requirements, but I really want to apply. Can they just be "to follow"?

A: We may process your applications as long as you submit the latest copy of your grades and the Appraisal Form accomplish by the previous teacher, adviser, or guidance counselor.

Q: How do we pay for the admission fee?

A: Wait for the Admission Staff to acknowledge your application thru email. The payment instructions will be given after the initial evaluation.

Q: How do we buy the uniform and books?

A: Due to suspension of face-to-face classes, uniforms and books are not required. Students will be advised during orientation regarding materials and attire.

Q: When is enrollment?

A: The schedule depends on the year/grade level of the students. Final schedule will be posted on our Facebook pages and the online platform.

Q: When is the start of classes?

A: Updates will be posted once information is available.
